Theory

Whitworth quick return mechanism is the inversion of a single slider crank chain. Single slider crank chain consists of three turning pairs & one sliding pair. The main aim is to convert rotary motion into reciprocating motion but reversed stroke is quicker in comparison with forwarding stroke. This mechanism is different from the crank and slotted lever quick return mechanism.
